Dia chi: , Thanh pho Ho Chi Minh
Thong tin ve Dia diem:
1. Quang truong Trung tam Thanh pho Di An
Day la quang truong cong cong chinh, nam o vi tri trung tam cua thanh pho:
Vi tri: Toa lac truoc khu vuc Trung tam Hanh chinh Cong Thanh pho Di An (gan Duong So 9, P. Di An).
Dien tich: Khoang 5.800 m².
Dac diem:
La cong trinh duoc xay dung theo phuong thuc xa hoi hoa, dua vao hoat dong tu dau nam 2020.
Cot co doc dao: Co mot Cot co Inox quay 360 do voi chieu cao 18 met, tich hop nhieu chuc nang hien dai nhu dieu khien tu xa, va he thong am thanh phat Quoc ca.
Tien ich: San quang truong rong, he thong chieu sang, cap nuoc tuoi cay tu dong, v.v.
Hoat dong:
La noi to chuc cac le mit tinh, su kien hoat dong lon cua Thanh pho Di An.
Moi chieu, nguoi dan va can bo, cong nhan vien chuc thuong den day de dao bo, vui choi, giai tri va tham gia cac hoat dong the duc the thao.

2. Khu Phuc Hop Quang truong Xanh (Green Square Di An City)
Day la mot du an khu do thi phuc hop lon, co quy mo hang chuc hecta va bao gom mot quang truong canh quan:
Vi tri: Nam tren truc Duong Quoc lo 1K, thuoc Phường Đong Hoa, TP. Di An.
Tong quan: La mot khu phuc hop gom cac toa thap can ho cao cap, nha pho thuong mai, biet thu va co khu Cong vien Trung tam voi canh quan dep.
Diem nhan: Khu quang truong voi khong gian cay xanh ket hop dai phun nuoc, tao canh quan tho mong hien dai, tao diem nhan cho khu do thi nay.
