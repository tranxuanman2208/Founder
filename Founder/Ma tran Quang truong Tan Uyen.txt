Ma tran Quang truong Tan Uyen
Dia chi: , Thanh pho Ho Chi Minh
Toa do: 8 1
Thong tin ve Dia diem:
1. Vi tri va Ten goi
Ten goi pho bien: Quang truong Thanh pho Tan Uyen (truoc day la Quang truong Thi xa Tan Uyen).
Dia chi: Nam tren duong DT747, thuoc phuong Uyen Hung, Thanh pho Tan Uyen, tinh Binh Duong.
Y nghia: Day la trung tam van hoa, chinh tri, va la mot trong nhung cong trinh ha tang do thi quan trong, gop phan thay doi dien mao cua thanh pho moi Tan Uyen.

2. Dac diem noi bat
Khong gian: Quang truong co dien tich lon, duoc thiet ke khang trang, hien dai, la noi to chuc cac su kien lon cua thanh pho va tinh.
Hoat dong:
Noi dien ra cac su kien trong dai nhu Le cong bo Nghi quyet thanh lap Thanh pho Tan Uyen.
Dia diem sinh hoat cong dong, vui choi, tap the duc cua nguoi dan dia phuong.
Vao buoi toi, quang truong thuong co anh sang lung linh, thu hut nguoi dan den thu gian.

3. Vi tri quan trong
Thanh pho Tan Uyen nam o phia Dong Nam tinh Binh Duong, co vi tri dia ly chien luoc, giap ranh voi TP. Bien Hoa (Dong Nai) va cac khu vuc phat trien khac cua Binh Duong. Quang truong nam ngay tai trung tam cua thanh pho, rat thuan tien cho viec giao thuong, ket noi va sinh hoat.
